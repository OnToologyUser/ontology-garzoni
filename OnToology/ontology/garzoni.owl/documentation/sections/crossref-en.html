Patronym 1c back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://vocab.dhlab.epfl.ch/garzoni#Patronymic1

Part of the name of a person which refers to one of his/her immediate relative. In (former) Italian family names, the patronymic is preceded by the word "di" or "quondam", e.g. "Federico Di Garla". "Di" stands for "figlio/figlia di" or "marito/moglie di" meaning the son/daughter or husband/wife of a living person. "Quondam" means the same but for a person who ceased to live.
has super-classes
Appellation Component c
